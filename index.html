Beagle

The Beagle is a breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound. The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling.

Trivia

  1. Beagles have an excellent sense of smell and tracking instinct.
  2. They are one of the most popular dog breeds in the United States.
  3. Beagles are intelligent but can be stubborn.
  4. Their ears help trap scent particles close to their nose.
